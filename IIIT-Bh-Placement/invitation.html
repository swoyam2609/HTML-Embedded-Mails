About the Institute

International Institute of Information Technology Bhubaneswar was established by the Government of Odisha in 2006. It is designed to be a premier Institute of Information Technology and related areas. The Institute was converted into a unitary university by the Government of Odisha on 20th January 2014. The Institute has its focus on quality and rigorous education, quality resources, academic infrastructure, technology, and innovation. These initiatives have helped IIIT-Bhubaneswar to achieve pre-eminence in India and beyond.

Admission Process

The admission process is designed to attract the best talent in our programmes.

  • The admission under the Open-to-all (“OTA”) category to regular B.Tech programmes will be purely based on the merit list from the JEE Main All India Ranks generated from the final NTA score published by NTA. The counseling shall be done by Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA)/ Central Seat Allocation Board (CSAB).

  • The Admission to M-Tech programme is open to students from across India. Institute uses GATE Scores as well as its own Entrance Test for admission to M-Tech Programme. In addition to the written test score, aptitude of the students is tested through Personal Interviews.
